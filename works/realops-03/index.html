Blind Codex audit / Secondary evidence

500 first-pass reviews. 140 required second reads.

Second-pass audit140 / 500

Model identity and machine grades were removed from the audit bundle. The first pass was sealed before reconciliation. All 47 disagreements plus a 20% profile/scenario-stratified sample received a second pass. Manual judgment never overrode executable or state-based evidence.

03Limits and next testWhat this pilot does and does not establish

Interpretation / Hard boundary

A routing result, not a universal leaderboard.

REALOPS-03 supports decisions for this private task mix. It does not prove general model superiority, future pricing, or performance under a different harness, tool budget, context, or topology.

  • 25 paired variants per profile/scenario remain a pilot sample.
  • EVI-01 and VIS-01 partly plateaued at the high end.
  • Estimated cost is a list-price model, not an invoice.
  • Recovered provider interruptions are recorded; they are not charged to model quality.
  • Raw prompts, fixtures, transcripts, workspaces, live paths, and grader internals remain private.

Next / Controlled swarm comparison

Hold the model fixed. Change the team.

The next campaign can keep Sol/high, these scenario contracts, and the same hard gates fixed while varying topology, communication, and total resource budget. That isolates whether a swarm adds evidence, not merely more tokens.
