Method / Before-state evaluation

Real work, hidden consequences.

Agents received hash-locked, time-cut research and repository states. Later evidence, contradictory measurements, and remote changes arrived only after the initial decision. Deterministic graders checked evidence binding, safe Git behavior, actual task completion, and revision under new information.

  1. 01
    Freeze the before-state

    Sanitized fixtures preserve the uncertainty the original work contained.

  2. 02
    Require an operational decision

    The agent must research, change, block, preserve, or release, not merely answer trivia.

  3. 03
    Reveal contradictory evidence

    The second phase measures revision rather than confident repetition.

  4. 04
    Grade state, provenance, and claims

    Primary validity comes from deterministic evidence, not an LLM judge.

Interpretation boundary

What this pilot does not establish

  • It is not a universal model-intelligence ranking.
  • Five runs per cell do not establish broad statistical superiority.
  • MRX-01 saturated at 5/5 for all four profiles.
  • Estimated list costs are tied to this frozen task mix and price snapshot.
  • Role labels do not remove the need for task-appropriate checks.

Sol / xhigh

No safer than high

Matched Sol/high at 4/5 RSP validity, cost 29% more, and added one catastrophic run.

Sol / ultra

Managed control

A provider-managed ceiling lane, excluded from the standard single-agent ranking and active routing roster.

Current routing rule

Default to Sol/high. Escalate when the cost of failure justifies Sol/max.

Luna/medium and Terra/high remain useful only where verification and review make their lower reliability an explicit, controlled trade.
