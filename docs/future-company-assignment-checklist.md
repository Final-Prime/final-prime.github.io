# Future company assignment checklist

This checklist records the intended transfer process. It is not itself an
assignment.

## Preconditions

- legal company name and registration number confirmed;
- governing jurisdiction confirmed;
- company bank, tax, and contracting details available;
- professional review obtained for high-value rights;
- complete asset and agreement inventory prepared.

## Assets to review

- copyright in code, websites, documentation, reviews, graphics, logos, video,
  art, games, schemas, and research materials;
- trademarks and service marks;
- domain names and social handles;
- GitHub organizations and repositories;
- email, hosting, cloud, signing, payment, and distribution accounts;
- patents, patent applications, invention records, and trade secrets;
- customer, contributor, contractor, and supplier agreements;
- data rights, model rights, and third-party licenses; and
- enforcement claims and existing permissions.

## Written transfer

The assignment should identify each asset class, territory, effective date,
consideration, warranties, retained rights, moral-right treatment where
applicable, further-assurance obligations, and the right to record the
transfer with relevant offices.

## After transfer

- update LICENSE, NOTICE, TRADEMARKS.md, legal pages, footers, and invoices;
- update registry ownership and correspondence addresses;
- update domain, GitHub, cloud, signing, and distribution accounts;
- preserve the signed chain of title;
- record assets intentionally retained personally; and
- update this checklist with the executed agreement reference.
