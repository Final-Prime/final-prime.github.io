## Locked research brief

The internal target is **controlled disorientation**: difficult to categorise, easy to understand, and impossible to dismiss as unserious.

- Primary route: `Explore selected work`.
- Target impressions: enigmatic, original, competent, independent, understanding, credible.
- Rejection impressions: confusing, amateur, arrogant, hostile, scam-like, generic.
- Audience cohorts: non-technical/general and business/technical.
- Brand constraints: dark base, cyan/fuchsia core, sharp industrial character, implicit rebellion, truthful founder-led voice.
- Public copy constraint: only the existing two-line motto is locked; all other hero copy remains provisional.

## Evidence rule

Standards and primary research outrank design-system guidance; design-system guidance outranks observational benchmarks. A benchmark can suggest a pattern but cannot prove that the pattern converts better.
