# Contribution policy

Final Prime does not currently accept unsolicited code, design, copy,
research, datasets, media, or other content contributions.

Do not submit confidential, proprietary, personal, client, security-sensitive,
patent-sensitive, or trade-secret material through an issue, pull request,
discussion, email, or public repository.

A contribution may be accepted only after:

1. the scope is requested in writing;
2. ownership and third-party provenance are documented;
3. the contributor confirms that the contribution is lawful to provide;
4. the applicable license or assignment terms are agreed in writing; and
5. the contribution passes security, privacy, editorial, and IP review.

Opening a pull request, issue, or discussion does not by itself transfer
ownership and does not create an obligation to review, accept, maintain, or
compensate the submission.

Security reports should follow SECURITY.md.
Permission requests should use finalprime.official@gmail.com.
