Thought publication protocol

  1. 01 / ThesisName the claim

    State what the piece is trying to establish.

  2. 02 / EvidenceShow the path

    Separate observation, inference and interpretation.

  3. 03 / BoundaryDeclare limits

    Identify spoilers, uncertainty and unpublished material.

  4. 04 / ReleasePublish when stable

    No fake cadence, title or date before the object is ready.

Editorial policy / Current

Research first. Verdict second.

Final Prime does not require every piece to use the same score, length or format. It requires the structure of the argument to be visible and the public claim to match the available evidence.
