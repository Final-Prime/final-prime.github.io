01

Thesis

The layer beneath the product

A product is a point in time.
The foundation should survive it.

Final Prime develops reusable mechanisms, coordination infrastructure, and operating methods that can support distinct products without rebuilding the same core capability.

The goal is not abstraction for its own sake. It is controlled reuse: clear boundaries, reviewable evidence, and technical foundations that become more valuable with each deployment.

What follows depends on what holds.
