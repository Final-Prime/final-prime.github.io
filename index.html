FINAL \ PRIME

Knowing the next move is survival.is survival. Understanding the game is control.is control.

Final Prime is an independent, cross-domain research and engineering effort by Daniel Kenessy. It pursues a more unified understanding of complex systems across software, applied philosophy, and physical reality.

Rather than treating visible symptoms, Final Prime traces them to the logic that makes them possible. It formulates and tests hypotheses. It develops theory and builds software on foundations designed so that the original problem no longer arises.
